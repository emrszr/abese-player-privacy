Privacy Policy for Abese Player

Last updated: [Add date]

Abese Player (“the App”) is a free IPTV playlist player that allows users to load and play their own M3U playlists. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Abese Player does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Diagnostics or crash data
  • Payment information
  • Advertising data

No data is sent to any server. No third-party services are integrated.

2. Playlist Data

Users may add M3U playlists in two ways:

  • By loading a file from the device’s local storage
  • By entering a playlist URL manually

All playlist data is stored only on the user’s device. We do not upload or transmit playlists to any server. Users are solely responsible for the content they load into the App.

3. No Login or Account

The App does not require registration, login, email, passwords, or social accounts. All functionality is local to the device.

4. No Internet Tracking or Analytics

Abese Player contains no ads, no trackers, and no analytics SDKs. The App does not monitor how you use it and does not connect to any external servers except when the user enters an M3U URL to load content.

5. Children’s Privacy

Since the App collects no personal information, it is safe for all ages.

6. Third-Party Content

The App does not provide or promote any IPTV content. Users must supply their own legally obtained playlists. We are not responsible for the content users choose to load.

7. Data Security

All user-added data is stored locally on the device and stays within the user’s control. If the user uninstalls the App, all data is deleted automatically.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published at the same link.
